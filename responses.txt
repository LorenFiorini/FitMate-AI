Workout Program - Week 1: Building a Strong Foundation

Day 1: Full-Body Strength

Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking or cycling)
Squats: 3 sets of 10 repetitions
Push-Ups: 3 sets of 8 repetitions
Planks: 3 sets of 20 seconds
Cool-down: 5 minutes of stretching
Day 2: Cardio and Core

Warm-up: 5 minutes of jumping jacks or jogging in place
Cardio (e.g., brisk walking, jogging, or cycling): 20 minutes
Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 12 repetitions per side
Leg Raises: 3 sets of 10 repetitions
Cool-down: 5 minutes of stretching
Day 3: Rest or Light Activity

Active rest day with light activities like walking or yoga to aid recovery.
Day 4: Upper Body Strength

Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cardio
Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 10 repetitions per arm
Dumbbell Chest Press: 3 sets of 8 repetitions
Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 repetitions
Tricep Dips (using a bench or stable surface): 3 sets of 10 repetitions
Cool-down: 5 minutes of stretching
Day 5: Cardio and Flexibility

Warm-up: 5 minutes of dynamic stretching
Cardio (e.g., swimming, cycling, or dancing): 20 minutes
Yoga or stretching routine: 15 minutes to improve flexibility
Cool-down: 5 minutes of deep breathing and relaxation
Day 6: Lower Body Strength

Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cardio
Lunges: 3 sets of 10 repetitions per leg
Leg Press (if available): 3 sets of 8 repetitions
Standing Calf Raises: 3 sets of 12 repetitions
Cool-down: 5 minutes of stretching
Day 7: Rest or Light Activity

Active rest day to allow your body to recover and prepare for the next week.
